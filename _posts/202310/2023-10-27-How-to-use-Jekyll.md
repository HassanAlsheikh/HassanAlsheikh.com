- You create posts (blog posts) in the _posts folder with a naming convention 

`YYYY-MM-DD-title-of-your-mark-down-file.md`

- make sure the **front mater** details are accurate cause #Jekyll takes its details from it for styling the post / page and its permalink and other information that you could integrate into your theme. 

- posts are organized in a `_posts` folder. Pages are in the root but you can create a folder for them and Jekyll will pick it up.
