# Scary Fast
Apple decided to launch their last event for this year today at 5pm USA time (PT) which is 4AM UAE time (GMT+4). The teasing title was Scary Fast and the logo animated to the "Finder" app which has a slogan "The Macintosh Desktop Experience". All tech social media companies and bloggers are predicting it is going to be something related to the M3 line up in the MacBook Pro series.
